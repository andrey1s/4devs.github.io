Website Development Process

From an idea to launch, we work together.

  • Site Discussion
    You describe us your site, its purposes, your vision. We consult you on a technical part of the question, provide possible solutions.
  • Specification Creation
    We discuss all details and work on user scenarios, write a detailed project specification.
  • Time and Cost Estimate
    Estimate the project by time and money using the detailed project specification.
  • Sign the Contract
    An individual contract creation. Sign it and split on milestones if needed.
  • Design + HTML Markup
    Creating a unique design. We constantly discuss it with you to provide the best possible result.
  • Active Development
    Our programmers implement the site. You can check the status on a dedicated demo server.
  • Testing and Quality Assurance
    Start a test period accordingly to the contract. All bugs will be fixed without extra cost.
  • Launch and Maintenance
    The Site is ready to solve your business tasks. We deploy it to the production server and provide support if needed.
